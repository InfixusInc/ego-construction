About Us

We are a dynamic and innovative construction company registered and incorporated in the republic of Zimbabwe, designed to provide solutions to every property. Ego Construction Company “ECC” was birthed after a realization that noble solutions were not being provided to the industry, and we have come as a pencil in the hand of an artisan with ego. We are a fusion of giants in the construction industry, with artisans who have more experience. Ego Construction is known of it’s affordability, friendliness, high quality, creativity, advocacy for space to all and being the construction company of choice.

Mission Statement

"To provide undoubted solutions in property development, construction of buildings, civil engineering services, classical housing amenities and property renovations with a heart beat to excellence, professionalism, integrity and oneness to all whilst advocating for space to everyone regardless of their backgrounds and creating homes within and across the region at the least possible costs."

Vision

"To be the leading champions in the realtor industry, holding solutions to every being through bringing in better amenities and property solutions with par excellence, highest sense of professionalism and integrity whilst thriving to grow our shareholders wealth to a multi-million business by the year 2020."
